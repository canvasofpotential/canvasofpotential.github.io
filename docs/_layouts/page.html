1 in 36 children are diagnosed with ASD each year.

As found in the CDC's Morbidity and Mortality Weekly Report, 2.8% of 8-year-old children have been identified to have Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD), with a 0.5% percent rise from 2018 report.
Canvas of Potential,
our student-run non-profit organization, is working to help those with ASD by providing a creative environment for them with art projects. We will be working under the guidance of a teacher specializing in art therapy to bring the best experience to those affected by ASD who attend our sessions.
